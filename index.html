school Doctoral Research
vibration Automatic Haptic Generation for Viewing Experiences

My doctoral research focuses on developing automatic haptic authoring methods that enrich user immersion in Virtual Reality (VR) and virtual environments. By analyzing audiovisual content with Multimodal AI, this work aims to translate digital media into meaningful, physically grounded haptic feedback, enabling users not just to see and hear but to feel virtual experiences.
