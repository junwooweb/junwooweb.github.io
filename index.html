# Human-Computer Interaction # Haptics # Artificial Intelligence # VR / AR / XR

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the department of Convergence IT Engineering at POSTECH, advised by Prof. Seungmoon Choi.

My research focuses on Physical AI and Immersive Interfaces. I aim to present enriched information to users by designing novel haptic hardware and generative rendering methods, bridging the gap between virtual intelligence and physical sensation.
