My doctoral research focuses on automatic haptic generation for multimedia content. I aim to build haptic-centered multimodal datasets. Based on these datasets, I am interested in developing joint multimodal AI models for audiovisual content understanding and haptic rendering, with the goal of enabling practical haptic applications.
